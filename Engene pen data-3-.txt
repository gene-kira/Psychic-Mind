⚠️ CONS — The real limitations
Even with all upgrades, some natural constraints remain:

Hard to maintain or extend long‑term.
2. GUI is single‑threaded

3. Persistent storage is append‑only
No indexing, querying, or pruning yet.
4. Worker pool is static
No dynamic scaling based on load.
5. Game telemetry is used lightly
It influences sensitivity but not deeper pipeline behavior.
6. No network transport
Capsules are created and decoded locally only.
7. No operator scripting console
You mentioned wanting this eventually.
8. No persistent ring state
Pressure history resets on restart.
