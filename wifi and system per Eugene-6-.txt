⚠️ CONS — What it does NOT do
✖ Not a full antivirus
No signature database.
No deep scanning.
No kernel hooks.

✖ Not a full firewall
Linux/macOS rules are suggestions, not automatic.

✖ Heuristic threat scoring
Good, but not ML-level intelligence.

✖ No sandboxing
It flags sandbox candidates but does not isolate them.

✖ GUI is functional, not beautiful
Operator-grade, not consumer-grade.

✖ Enforcement can be aggressive
False positives can kill legitimate processes.

✖ Coordinator is a single point of failure
If it goes down, swarm still works, but loses global coordination.

✖ No encrypted swarm traffic yet
HTTP only — LAN-safe, but not WAN-safe.
