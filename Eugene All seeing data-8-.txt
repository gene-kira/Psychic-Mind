⚠️ CONS
The honest limitations:

❌ High CPU usage on busy systems
Process scanning + network scanning + GUI refresh = heavy.

❌ eBPF + ETW are stubs
Real probes require root/admin and custom code.

❌ Not kernel‑level by default
psutil is user‑space → limited depth.

❌ Firewall blocking is basic
IP‑level only, no domain/port rules.

❌ LAN scanning uses ARP
Simple, may miss VLANs or stealth hosts.

❌ Single‑file = huge codebase
Harder to maintain than modular packages.

❌ GUI and daemon share process
If GUI crashes, daemon continues — but GUI must be restarted manually.

❌ Swarm sync is basic
No encryption, no consensus, no conflict resolution.
